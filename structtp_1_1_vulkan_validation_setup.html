tp::VulkanValidationSetup struct

Describes whether Vulkan validation should be enabled, optionally with control over its specific features.

Contents

  • Reference
    • Constructors, destructors, conversion operators

Remark

To be able to report the validation messages to the application, the tp::ApplicationExtension::EXT_DebugUtils extension must be present and requested.

This is independent of Tephra specific validation, which can be enabled through the TEPHRA_ENABLE_DEBUG_TEPHRA_VALIDATION preprocessor define.
