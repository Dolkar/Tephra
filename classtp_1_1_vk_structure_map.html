template<typename TStructureTypeTrait>
tp::VkStructureMap class

A heterogenous container of unique Vulkan structure types. The structures get zero initialized with sType filled out appropriately and pNext used to chain them in the order they were added.

Contents

  • Reference
    • Public functions

Remark

Not all Vulkan structure types are allowed to be used in this map. They must either be included in tephra/vulkan/structures.hpp or as one of the special cases in this class.
