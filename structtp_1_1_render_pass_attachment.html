tp::RenderPassAttachment struct

Represents an tp::ImageView as a render pass attachment, allowing it to be bound as a render target inside of its subpasses. Also specifies any operations that are to be done at the beginning or end of the render pass. Clearing an image through an attachment load operation tends to be more efficient than with an explicit command.

Parameters
